How It Works

Our Payment Collection Process

A straightforward process designed to ensure timely payments and complete transparency.

1. Rent Schedule

We establish a clear rent schedule with your tenants, specifying payment amounts and due dates.

2. Payment Reminders

Automated reminders are sent to tenants before rent is due to ensure timely payments.

3. Flexible Collection

Tenants can pay through multiple convenient methods including online, direct deposit, or auto-pay.

4. Payment Verification

Our team verifies all payments and follows up on any late or missing payments promptly.

5. Owner Disbursement

Rental income is transferred to your account quickly and securely once processed.

6. Financial Reporting

Detailed monthly statements show all income, expenses, and fees for complete transparency.
