FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tenant services.

How do I submit a maintenance request?

You can submit maintenance requests through your online tenant portal, by calling our office during business hours, or by using our maintenance request form on our website. For emergencies, please call our 24/7 emergency line.

What payment methods do you accept for rent?

We accept online payments through the tenant portal (credit/debit card or bank transfer), automatic payments, personal checks, money orders, and cashier's checks. Cash is not accepted for security reasons.

How do I renew my lease?

We typically contact tenants 60-90 days before their lease expires with renewal options. You can indicate your preference to renew through your tenant portal or by contacting your property manager directly.

What is your pet policy?

Pet policies vary by property. Generally, approved pets require a pet deposit and/or monthly pet rent. Service animals are exempt from pet fees but still require documentation. Please check your specific property's policy.

How do I report an emergency after hours?

For emergencies that threaten life, property, or essential services (like no heat in winter), call our 24/7 emergency line at (123) 456-7890. For non-emergencies, please use the tenant portal or wait until business hours.

When will I get my security deposit back?

Security deposits are typically processed within 30 days of move-out, following a thorough inspection of the property. Any deductions will be itemized and included with your refund.
