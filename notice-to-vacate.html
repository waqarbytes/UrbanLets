Move-Out Process

Vacating Your Rental

Follow these steps for a smooth move-out experience.

1. Submit Notice

Complete the notice to vacate form at least 30 days before your intended move-out date as required by your lease.

2. Receive Confirmation

We'll review your notice and provide confirmation along with detailed move-out instructions and cleaning checklist.

3. Schedule Inspection

Arrange for a move-out inspection to be conducted after you have vacated and cleaned the property.

4. Clean the Property

Thoroughly clean the property according to the provided checklist to avoid cleaning charges.

5. Return Keys

Return all keys, access cards, and remotes to our office on or before your move-out date.

6. Receive Deposit

Your security deposit will be processed within 30 days with an itemized list of any deductions.

Move-Out Cleaning Checklist

Use this checklist to ensure you leave the property in good condition.

General

  • Remove all personal belongings and furniture
  • Patch and spackle nail holes in walls
  • Clean all light fixtures and replace burnt-out bulbs
  • Clean all windows, tracks, and windowsills
  • Dust all blinds and wash curtains if applicable
  • Vacuum all carpets thoroughly
  • Sweep and mop all hard surface floors
  • Clean all baseboards and door frames
  • Remove all trash and debris from the property

Kitchen

  • Clean oven inside and out, including racks and drip pans
  • Clean stovetop, burners, and control knobs
  • Clean microwave inside and out
  • Clean refrigerator/freezer inside and out, and leave running
  • Clean all countertops and backsplashes
  • Clean sink, faucet, and drain
  • Clean all cabinets and drawers inside and out
  • Clean dishwasher inside and out, including filters
  • Clean range hood and filter

Bathrooms

  • Clean toilet thoroughly including base and behind
  • Clean bathtub/shower including doors/curtains and remove soap scum
  • Clean sink, countertops, and cabinets
  • Clean mirror and medicine cabinet
  • Clean all tile and grout
  • Clean exhaust fan cover

Exterior & Misc.

  • Remove all items from patio/balcony/yard
  • Sweep patio/balcony area
  • Mow lawn and trim bushes (if applicable)
  • Remove all trash from exterior of property
  • Clean out garage/storage areas
  • Replace air filters
  • Replace smoke detector batteries if needed

Final Steps

  • Take photos of the clean property for your records
  • Return all keys, garage openers, access cards, etc.
  • Provide forwarding address for security deposit return
  • Notify utility companies of your move-out date
  • Update your address with USPS

Important Note:

Properties must be cleaned to a professional standard. If the property requires additional cleaning after you vacate, appropriate charges will be deducted from your security deposit. For optimal results, consider hiring a professional cleaning service.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about moving out and security deposits.

When will I receive my security deposit?

Security deposits are processed within 30 days of your move-out date. You will receive an itemized statement showing any deductions along with your refund, which will be sent to your provided forwarding address.

What if I need to break my lease early?

If you need to terminate your lease before the end date, please contact us immediately. Early termination typically requires payment of a lease break fee as specified in your lease agreement, plus rent until a new tenant is found.

Do I need to have the carpets professionally cleaned?

This depends on your lease agreement. Some leases require professional carpet cleaning upon move-out, while others only require thorough vacuuming. Check your lease or contact us to confirm. If professional cleaning is required, please provide a receipt.

What if I discover damage during move-out cleaning?

If you discover damage beyond normal wear and tear, document it with photos and inform us immediately. Being proactive often results in lower repair costs than if the damage is discovered during inspection.

Can I use my security deposit for my last month's rent?

No, security deposits cannot be used as rent payment. You must pay your rent through the end of your lease or notice period. Security deposits are held to cover potential damages or unpaid balances after you vacate.

What happens if I don't give proper notice?

If you fail to provide the required notice period (typically 30 days), you may be charged for rent through the notice period even after you've vacated. Always provide notice in writing according to your lease terms.
