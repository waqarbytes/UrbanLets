FAQ

Room Rental FAQs

Common questions about finding a room in Auckland.

What's included in the room price?

Room prices typically include rent and utilities (power, water, internet), but this varies by property. We always specify what's included in each listing, and you can filter rooms by what's included in the price.

How does the viewing process work?

After we receive your application, we'll contact you with suitable room options. If you're interested, we'll arrange viewings at times that work for you and the current tenants. You'll have the opportunity to see the room, common areas, and meet potential flatmates.

Is a bond required?

Yes, most rooms require a bond (usually 2-4 weeks' rent) plus 1-2 weeks' rent in advance. The bond is held by Tenancy Services and returned at the end of your tenancy, provided there's no damage or unpaid rent.

Can international students or workers apply?

Absolutely! We welcome applications from international students and workers. Many of our rooms are perfect for newcomers to Auckland, and we can help guide you through the process if you're unfamiliar with NZ rental procedures.

How long does it take to find a room?

Most applicants find a suitable room within 1-2 weeks. The timeframe depends on your budget, location preferences, and room availability. We recommend applying at least 2-3 weeks before your desired move-in date.

Are bills included in the rent?

This varies by property. Some include all bills (power, water, internet), while others charge bills separately or have a bills contribution amount on top of rent. Each listing specifies the arrangement.
