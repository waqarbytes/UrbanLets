FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our property management services.

What are your management fees?

Our management fees range from 6% to 10% of the monthly rental income, depending on the level of service you choose and the type of property. Contact us for a personalized quote.

How do you screen potential tenants?

We conduct thorough background checks including credit history, rental history, employment verification, income verification, and reference checks to ensure we find reliable tenants.

How quickly can you find tenants for my property?

On average, we place qualified tenants within 2-3 weeks. Our extensive marketing reach and tenant database allow us to match properties with suitable tenants quickly and efficiently.

How are maintenance issues handled?

We have a network of trusted contractors who provide quality work at competitive rates. For minor issues, we handle them promptly without bothering you. For larger repairs, we'll consult with you before proceeding.

What happens if a tenant doesn't pay rent?

We have robust rent collection procedures. If a tenant fails to pay, we immediately begin the follow-up process according to legal requirements. Our premium and complete packages also include eviction protection and rent guarantees.

How often will I receive financial statements?

Monthly financial statements are provided to all owners on our premium and complete plans. These include income, expenses, and any upcoming maintenance needs. You'll also have 24/7 access to your owner portal for real-time information.
