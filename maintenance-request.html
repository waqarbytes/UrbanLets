Our Maintenance Process

A simple and efficient process to address your maintenance needs.

1. Submit Request

Fill out the maintenance request form with details about the issue, photos if available, and preferred access times.

2. Confirmation

Receive a confirmation of your request with a tracking number and estimated response timeline.

Common Maintenance Issues

Tips and information about common household maintenance problems.

Plumbing Issues

For minor clogs, try using a plunger before submitting a request. If you experience a major leak, locate your water shut-off valve and turn it off immediately, then submit an emergency maintenance request.

HVAC Problems

Check if your thermostat has fresh batteries and is set to the correct mode. Ensure air filters are clean and vents aren't blocked by furniture. If the system isn't working properly after these checks, submit a maintenance request.

Electrical Issues

If you experience a power outage, check if it affects just your unit or the entire building. Check circuit breakers before submitting a request. Never attempt to fix electrical issues yourself - this is dangerous and violates most lease agreements.

Appliance Malfunctions

Check that appliances are properly plugged in and that circuit breakers haven't tripped. For refrigerators, ensure vents aren't blocked and coils are dust-free. For dishwashers, clear any food debris from filters.

Lock and Key Issues

If you're locked out during business hours, contact our office. After hours, contact our emergency line. There may be a fee for lockout services. For damaged locks, submit a maintenance request immediately.

Pest Control

Keep your unit clean and store food in sealed containers to prevent pests. Report any pest sightings immediately. We provide regular preventative pest control in most properties, and can schedule additional treatments if needed.
