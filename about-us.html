About Urban Lets

Our Story

Urban Lets : to transform the property management industry by creating exceptional experiences for both property owners and tenants.

At Urban Lets, we’re passionate about creating a better rental experience for everyone. We believe finding a place to live shouldn’t be stressful, and having roommates shouldn’t feel like a gamble. That’s why we built a platform that connects people with compatible roommates and streamlines the rental process for both tenants and landlords.
